Music

I've been making music in various formats since I was 14. My first long-term band position was bass player for The Circadian Kingdom, lead by Charlotte Sipprelle (other members of that band were Kiaran McMillan, Stefan Lake, and Nav Ganti). Charlotte and I went on to do a duo project, The Ink Pneumites, which also involved Graham Stark, Hugo Wong, Seth Opderhyde, and Kailan Oxford. The Ink Pneumites still exist, to some extent, though it's just me and Charlotte now.

I also have an active solo project, kickedabear. I released an album in 2019, and I happily accept mixing/mastering contracts.
